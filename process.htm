The AIAPM Methodology

From Algorithm
to Installation

A fully integrated pipeline engineered by Studio Luminant. Every panel begins with computational design and ends with a certified, installed surface — with total in-house control at every stage.

01
Step 01 — Design

Parametric
Design Brief

Every project starts with a computational design brief. We translate the architect's spatial intent — pattern language, depth, geometry — into parametric constraints that drive every downstream decision. BIM-ready outputs are included as standard.

02
Step 02 — Modelling

AIAPM
Digital Modelling

Our proprietary AI-Assisted Product Modelling pipeline converts the design brief into high-fidelity 3D surface models. Iteration is fast and non-destructive — changes at this stage cost nothing. The model drives the mould directly, eliminating manual translation error.

03
Step 03 — Moulding

Precision
Master Mould

CNC-machined master moulds are produced directly from the digital model. Silicone or polyurethane production moulds are cast from the master, enabling unlimited reproduction runs without geometric degradation. Mould costs are absorbed across the production volume.

04
Step 04 — Casting

Vertical
In-House Casting

Lumina PMAG™ (Polymer Modified Alpha Gypsum) and Lumina PUCOMP™ panels are cast vertically in our Sakarya facility under controlled conditions. Alpha gypsum produces a denser, harder surface than beta-grade alternatives. Each batch is tested for compressive strength and surface integrity before release.

05
Step 05 — Finishing

Automotive-Grade
2K Finish

Every panel is finished using a two-component automotive coating system — the same technology applied to luxury vehicle exteriors. The result is a micro-smooth surface in any RAL or custom colour, resistant to humidity, UV, and impact. Custom textures, metallic effects, and patinas are available.

Manufacturing HQ

Made in
Sakarya

Our 2,400 m² facility in Sakarya / Sapanca houses the full production pipeline under one roof — from CNC moulding through casting to finishing and QA. Vertical integration eliminates sub-contractor delays and gives us total quality control from brief to crate.

Every panel ships with a certified material data sheet and installation drawing. Logistics and on-site installation support available to the Middle East, Caucasus, and Eastern Europe.
