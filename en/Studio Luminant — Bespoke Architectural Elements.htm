How We Work

Brief to Installation,
Under One Roof.

One in-house pipeline — design, moulding, casting, finishing. Built to compress timelines and take the specification risk out of large-scale relief.

01
Design & Modelling
From Brief to 3D Model

Send a sketch, a reference, or a brief. Our AI-Assisted Product Modelling (AIAPM) process turns it into a high-resolution 3D surface model within days — texture and depth standard fabricators can't hold. Iteration is digital, so changes here cost nothing. BIM-ready from the start.

02
Moulding & Casting
3D-Printed Masters, Precision Casting

We 3D-print the mould master straight from the model, then vacuum-cast each panel under controlled conditions in Lumina PMAG™ or Lumina PUCOMP™ — every batch checked for surface integrity before it leaves the floor.

03
Finishing & Delivery
Artisan Finish, Ready to Install

Each panel is finished by hand — any RAL or custom colour, with cold-cast metal and textured options — then crated with its technical data sheet, installation drawing, and BIM file.

    What to Know
    Before You Specify.

    What are your lead times?

    Most projects run 8–14 weeks from brief to delivery, with a physical sample in the first few weeks. The exact figure is scaled to your project and confirmed in the quote.

    Lead Times →
    How heavy are the panels — do they need special support?

    Panels are formulated to be lightweight; most applications use standard mounting systems. Structural data is included in the specification package.

    Weight & Structural →
    Is there a minimum project size?

    We work from a single feature wall to full-floor cladding. For very small jobs we assess fit together first.

    Do you provide BIM and specification files?

    Yes — BIM objects and technical data sheets are ready at specification stage, with no last-minute surprises.

    Specification Guide →
    How different is this to specify and install?

    Not very. If your practice already specifies GRC or GRG, you specify Lumina PMAG™ the same way — the same kind of fixings, the same substrate detailing. Our specification guide gives you clauses and fixing details to drop straight into your tender.

    Specification Guide →
    Do you handle installation?

    Every panel ships with installation drawings and details for your contractor. Where you need us on site, we quote installation per project — it isn't bundled into the panel price. Panels are supplied EXW Sakarya by default.
