Unified Design System

Design once,
deploy everywhere.

Walls, ceilings, columns, and feature elements usually come from separate vendors — each with its own tolerances, lead times, and finish variance, and the coordination falls to you. The AIAPM (AI-Assisted Product Modelling) master is material-agnostic; the geometry is held in one digital master and one silicone mould. Only the casting material changes between surfaces — so spatial coherence is engineered in from the start, not chased on site.
