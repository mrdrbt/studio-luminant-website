Process

From Concept
to Installation

Our AI-Assisted Product Modelling (AIAPM) process compresses months of design development into weeks.

01
Design Brief
Architects share their vision — reference images, spatial constraints, brand language. We translate intent into parametric geometry.
02
AI Modelling
Our AIAPM pipeline generates pattern variations, runs structural simulations, and outputs production-ready 3D models and BIM objects.
03
Sample & Approve
Physical sample produced in chosen material and finish. Architect reviews relief depth, texture, colour under real lighting conditions.
04
Production
Full batch casting, quality-controlled artisan surface finishing, packaged for site delivery and installation.
